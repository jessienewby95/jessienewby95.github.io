The Purpose of This Website

I designed this website for my Writing and Digital Media class at Virginia Tech. Over the course of the semester, my group and myself worked together to better enahnce our skills in both writing and creating digital presentations, such as a podcast and a video. While the semester was fun, yet stressful, I am proud of my group and the work we accomplished. Our podcast consists of the six of us playing and reviewing board games to determine if they were still in style (thyre' not), whereas our video takes the viewers on a virtual tour of the Moss Arts Center, located on the Virginia Tech campus. The newsletters were a creative way for the group to discuss various topics related to music and the arts. For instance, I tended to base my posts on various bands that I listen to, whereas other members chose to discuss pieces of art or memes that were popular on the Internet.
